Info and Resources


These three following videos cover three of the main topics that make up the bulk of the 1st semester of Physics 1. The main topics are, Newton's Laws of Motion, Kinematics in 1 Dimension, and Kinematics in 2 Dimensions. Each video is handpicked from many to be the best of the best to learn about the topic, learn the questions under them, and learn how to answer them. Each video also helps you figure out how to use these topics far beyond what is simply remembered.


Every new major lesson will have one new video allocated to that lesson, so the student is constantly updated with live time Physics help. Alongside each major lesson, if an interesting topic is seen in class, this will then also be allocated one video, to provide students not only help with major lessons, but fun ones as well, and if a new difficult lesson is encountered, then a new video shall be allocated. PhysiForm+, for students, by students.
