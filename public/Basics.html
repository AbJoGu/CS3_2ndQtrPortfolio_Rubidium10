Introduction

(Real-life example)

A metal pellet is fired from a pellet gun at ground level, at an angle of 40 Degrees (θ). the pellet is able to reach a target. At a velocity of 400 meters per second (v), what is the horizontal distance the pellet travels (R)? Ignore air resistance.




(Formula)

Range is equal to

| R = [v²sin(2θ)]/g |

Therefore, if you substitute the values, you will end up with

| R = [400²sin(2x40)]/9.8 |

| R ≈ 16 080 m|
